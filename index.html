01 · About

Engineer. Researcher. Builder.

I'm a graduate student at UC San Diego, working at the intersection of probabilistic robotics, control theory, and machine learning. My research in the MURO Lab focuses on Model Predictive Path Integral (MPPI) control and Distributed Gaussian Variational Inference (DGVI) for safe, real-time multi-agent systems.

My dual background in Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering gives me a unique lens for tackling problems in autonomous vehicles, robot navigation, and AI-driven safety — bridging theory and real-world deployment.

Actively seeking roles in autonomy & AV validation, AI engineering, and robotics software where I can push the boundaries of trustworthy autonomous systems.
